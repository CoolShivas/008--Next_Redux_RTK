Que. 3.) In which way the data flow in redux.

StoreJs contains the data which we are going to push inside the app.

Que. 4.) What is Slice ?
Ans. 4.) A slice is a collection of Redux reducer logic and actions for a single feature in your app. In slice we defined action and reducer in a single file.
